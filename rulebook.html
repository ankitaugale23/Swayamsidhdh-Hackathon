स्वयंसिद्ध HACKATHON 2020

Rules

  • The technical project will depend on Covid-19 issues/problems or problems faced after lockdown.
  • Problem should be self identified and should give technical solution.
  • Create your own project; do not copy other's project.
  • A group should have minimum 1 member and maximum 5.
  • Entries more than one is allowed i.e. solutions more than one can be submitted.
  • Unique team name should be provided.
  • Participants will have to present the PPT on zoom app or on other online platform after selection of the project.
  • Presentation should be done according to the submitted template+video+prototype.
  • After selection of best idea the process of working will be started after lockdown when colleges will reopen.
  • If the present situation of the pandemic doesn’t improve then the final round will also be conducted online, the responsibility regarding the changes will be taken by the institution or the event coordinator
  • All participants will be provided with the certificate.
  • No registration fees, registration is for free.
  • Copyright:-The selected project can be implemented by the institution (AISSMSCOE,Pun, for rural or urban development or any other purpose. But the project ownership will remain with the participants.

Rules Regarding Presentation

  • The presentation will be conducted among the participants whose solution is been selected.
  • The dates regarding the presentation will be updated on the website.
  • Selected participants will have to present the PPT on zoom app
  • Presentation should be done according to the submitted template+video+prototype.
  • After selection of best idea the process of working will be started after lockdown when colleges will reopen.
  • If the present situation of the pandemic doesn’t improve then the final round will also be conducted online,the responsibility regarding the changes will be taken by the institution or the event coordinator.
  • All team members should be present on the time for the presentation, it will be monitored strictly.
  • All teams should follow the given sequences and time 10+5 min compulsory.
  • Before proceeding the presentation all team members are requested to open their video and if required they can unmute themselves.
  • Other teams should off their microphone and video when 1 team is presenting their presentation.
  • Any one or all participants can present the presentation; time limitation should be strictly followed.
  • Teams should not share their information such as city, state, name of institute at the time of presentation.
  • Only jury members will ask questions to the presenter.
  • Participants may leave the meeting once they are finished with their presentation.
  • E-certificates will only be provided to those participants who have presented their presentation.
